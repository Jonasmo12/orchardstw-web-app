School Policy.

  • The school starts at 07h00 for educators and 07h25 for learners and ends at 14h30 for teachers and 13h00 for learners, unless there are compelling reasons for such times to be extended and/or reduced.
  • The Administration Office shall be open from 07h00 until 15h30.

  • Every class educator shall complete all the learners details in the attendence register at the beginning of every term.
  • Every class educator shall complete the register on a daily basis and take note of all the absentess during the register period.
  • On Fridays, the attendance registers shall be completed for the weeks attendance and sent to the office before 09h00 to enable the Administrative staff to complete and consolidate the weeks attendance.
  • All the letters of absenteeism and doctor certificates shall be filed in learner's profile.
  • At the end of the year, all attendance registers shall be sent to the office for safekeeping.

  • Excursions shall be applied for at the District as per department directives.
  • Educators must have signed permission/consent from in their possession, in which the parents grant their permission for the learner to go on an arranged excursion.
  • All requests for transport must be directed to the Principal.
